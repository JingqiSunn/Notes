#### Example Problem #1

> Having known $P(A)+P(B)=0.5$ and $P(A\bar B)+P(\bar A B)= 0.3$.
>
> Ask $P(AB)$.

*Solution:*
$$
P(A)+P(B)=0.5
$$

$$
P(A\bar B)+P(\bar A B)= 0.3
$$

$$
P(A\or B)=P(A)+P(B)-P(AB)
$$

$$
P(A\or B)=P(AB)+P(\bar A B)+P(A\bar B)
$$

$$
P(A)+P(B)-P(AB)=P(AB)+P(\bar A B)+P(A\bar B)
$$

$$
P(AB)=0.1
$$
