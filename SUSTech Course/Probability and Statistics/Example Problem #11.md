#### Example Problem #11

> $P(AB)=P(\bar A \bar B)$, $P(A)=p$,
>
> Ask $P(B)$.

*Solution:*
$$
P(AB)=P(\bar A\bar B)
$$

$$
P(\bar A \or \bar B) = P(A\or B)
$$

$$
P(\bar A)+ P(\bar B )-P(\bar A\bar B)=P(A)+P(B)-P(AB)
$$

$$
P(\bar A)+ P(\bar B )=P(A)+P(B)
$$

$$
P(A)=p
$$

$$
1-p+(1-P(B))=p+P(B)
$$

$$
P(B)=1-p
$$
